SECTION 4: DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Local Data Storage

The majority of your User Data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: Energy log entries, hourly statistics, and AI-generated schedules
  • SharedPreferences: Application settings, user preferences, and session data

This local-first architecture means your personal energy data remains on your device and is not uploaded to or stored on Company servers.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted Authentication: All authentication flows utilize industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure API Communication: All network communications with third-party services use encrypted connections
  • Local Data Isolation: Each user's local data is isolated and accessible only through authenticated sessions
  • No Plain-Text Credential Storage: Authentication credentials are never stored in plain text on your device

4.3 Security Limitations

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

SECTION 5: DATA RETENTION

5.1 Energy Data Retention

  • Default Retention Period: Energy logs and statistics are retained for thirty-five (35) days by default.
  • Configurable Retention: You may adjust the retention period through the Application settings.
  • Automatic Deletion: Data older than your configured retention period is automatically purged from local storage.

5.2 Account Data Retention

  • Your Firebase authentication session persists until you explicitly sign out.
  • Account-related data is retained for the duration of your active use of the Application.

5.3 Deletion Upon Request

You may delete your data at any time through:

  • Settings - Clear Data: Removes all local energy data, statistics, and preferences.
  • Sign Out: Terminates your authentication session.
  • Uninstallation: Removes the Application and all associated local data from your device.
  • Account Deletion Request: Contact us to request complete deletion of all data associated with your account.
